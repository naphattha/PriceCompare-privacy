How to Delete Your Data

Since Price Compare stores all data locally on your device, deleting your data is simple and instant:

Option 1: Delete Individual Comparisons

1 Open the Price Compare app

2 Go to the "History" tab

3 Tap on any comparison entry to expand it

4 Tap the 🗑️ delete button

5 Confirm deletion

Option 2: Clear All History

1 Open the Price Compare app

2 Go to the "History" tab

3 Tap "ล้างทั้งหมด" (Clear All) button at the top

4 Confirm that you want to delete all history

Option 3: Uninstall the App

1 On your Android device, long-press the Price Compare app icon

2 Select "Uninstall" or drag to "Uninstall" area

3 Confirm uninstallation

⚠️ Important: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your comparison data from your device. This action cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete data or uninstall the app, the following information is removed:

  • All saved comparison history
  • Product names, prices, and quantities you entered
  • App settings and preferences

ℹ️ Remember: We don't have any of your data on our servers, so there's nothing to delete from our end. Everything is on your device only.

No Account Required

Price Compare doesn't require you to create an account, so there's no account to delete. You never provided us with:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Personal information
  • Login credentials

This means your privacy is protected from the start!

After Deletion

After you delete your data or uninstall the app:

  • ✅ All local data is immediately and permanently removed
  • ✅ No traces remain on your device (except standard Android system logs)
  • ✅ You can reinstall the app anytime and start fresh
  • ✅ No data recovery is possible (we don't have backups)

Reinstalling the App

You can reinstall Price Compare anytime from the Google Play Store. When you reinstall:

  • You'll start with a clean slate
  • No previous data will be restored (we don't store backups)
  • The app will work exactly as it did when you first installed it
