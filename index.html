Privacy Policy

Price Compare App

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Price Compare! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Price Compare is a simple utility app that helps you compare product prices by calculating price per unit. Your privacy is our top priority.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

Price Compare is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • ✗ No personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • ✗ No location data
  • ✗ No device information
  • ✗ No analytics or tracking data
  • ✗ No advertising identifiers
  • ✗ No user account information

2.2 Data Storage

All data you enter in the app (product names, prices, quantities, comparison history) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use, share, or process any of your data. The app functions entirely offline using local storage on your device.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any data because we don't collect any data.

Price Compare does not:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Sell your information
  • Use analytics services
  • Display advertisements that track you
  • Connect to external servers (except for optional donation feature)

5. Data Security

Your comparison data is stored securely on your device using Android's built-in secure storage mechanisms. Only you have access to this data through the app.

Since all data is stored locally, your privacy is protected even if our servers were compromised (which is not applicable since we don't have servers).

6. Your Data Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Delete: You can delete individual comparisons or clear all history within the app
  • Export: Currently not supported, but may be added in future versions
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently

6.2 Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the Price Compare app. This will permanently remove all locally stored comparison data from your device.

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 No Third-Party Services

Price Compare does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7.2 Optional Donation Feature

The app includes an optional "Feed Us" donation feature using PromptPay. If you choose to use this feature:

  • You will be shown a PromptPay QR code
  • Any transaction is handled entirely by your banking app
  • We do not receive any information about whether you made a donation or the amount
  • This feature is completely optional and does not affect app functionality

8. Children's Privacy

Price Compare does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages to use.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the app (for significant changes)
  • Updating the version on our GitHub repository

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app works the same way regardless of your location.

11. Contact Us

12. Open Source

Price Compare is committed to transparency. While the app code is currently not open source, this Privacy Policy is publicly available and we welcome community feedback.

Summary: Price Compare respects your privacy completely. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All your data stays on your device, and you have full control over it. That's our promise to you.
