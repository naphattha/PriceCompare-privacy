## 📱 About Price Compare

Price Compare is a simple utility app that helps you compare product prices by calculating price per unit. Your privacy is our top priority.

## 🔐 Privacy Highlights

- ✅ **No data collection** - We don't collect any personal information
- ✅ **Local storage only** - All data stays on your device
- ✅ **No tracking** - No analytics or advertising trackers
- ✅ **No accounts** - No signup or login required
- ✅ **Complete control** - Delete your data anytime
